Privacy Policy

At GT Mall, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your data when you visit our website gtmall.run.place.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address and payment details when you place an order, create an account or contact us.

2. Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate with you about your orders and support requests
  • Improve our website, products and services
  • Send promotional offers (only if you have opted in)

3. Payment Information

We use trusted third-party payment gateways (such as Razorpay) to process payments. We do not store your full card details on our servers.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not work properly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with service providers (courier partners, payment gateways) only for order processing and delivery.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to update or correct your personal information or to request deletion where applicable.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
