Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 November 2025

1. Information we collect

When you create an account or place an order on GT Mall, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address and order details. Basic technical data such as IP address and browser type may also be collected for security and analytics.

2. How we use your information
  • To create and manage your GT Mall account
  • To process orders, payments and refunds
  • To send important order updates and notifications
  • To improve our website and customer support
3. Payments

GT Mall uses trusted third-party payment gateways (such as Razorpay or Cashfree) to process your payments. Your full card/UPI details are handled securely by the payment provider and are not stored on our servers.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your cart and improve website performance. You can clear or disable cookies from your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers such as delivery partners, payment gateways and analytics tools, only for operating and improving GT Mall.
